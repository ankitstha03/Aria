Why Aria?

The users of Aria can listen to any music available in theapplication and create their own custom playlists to share withthe world. The users can also listen to playlists of other users todiscover new music.

The playlist can be upvoted by users andthe top playlists will be featured in the application. The results will be published atthe end of each week and new top songs and playlists will befeatured for the next week
